2. Scope

This Policy applies to the Waddle mobile app, our websites (e.g., waddle.app), and related services (support, notifications, subscriptions).

3. Data we collect

  • Identity & account: name, email, authentication identifiers (Sign in with Apple/Google or email/password), profile photo if provided.
  • App data: expenses, transfers, categories, recurrences, currency, preferences, relationships/sharing (e.g., partner), attachments you upload.
  • Subscriptions & payments: technical purchase info (product IDs, receipts/tokens, subscription status) — transactions are handled by Apple App Store / Google Play, not by Waddle.
  • Devices & diagnostics: device model, OS, notification tokens, logs, performance metrics, crash reports.
  • Support: your messages and any attachments.
  • Location: only if you grant permission and a feature needs it (e.g., currency suggestions); otherwise not collected.

We do not collect your bank card details. If you connect to a third-party banking aggregator (e.g., Powens), data is processed under their policies and your specific authorizations.

4. Sources of data

  • Directly from you: when creating your account, using the app, or contacting support.
  • Platforms: Apple App Store / Google Play for purchase validation and subscription status.
  • Processors: authentication, notifications, database, analytics providers (see Section 6).

5. Purposes & legal bases

Legal bases (GDPR Art. 6)

Contract, Legitimate interest, Consent, Legal obligation.

  • Provide the service (contract): create/maintain your account, sync your data, and deliver features (expenses, transfers, recurrences, multi-currency).
  • Subscriptions (contract/legal obligation): enable purchase, validate eligibility, manage status and billing via Apple/Google.
  • Security & integrity (legitimate interest/obligation): fraud/abuse prevention, diagnostics, logging.
  • Notifications (legitimate interest/consent depending on platform): reminders, alerts, service messages; you can manage preferences in your OS.
  • Product improvement & stats (legitimate interest/consent): anonymized/aggregated measurement; where required we ask for your consent.
  • Legal compliance (obligation): responding to lawful requests.

6. Sharing & processors

We do not sell your data. We share it only with:

  • Technical providers (hosting, database, auth, analytics, crash) — e.g., Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions), Crashlytics/Analytics.
  • Subscription management — Apple App Store, Google Play (purchases) and RevenueCat (subscription mediation/validation).
  • Push notifications — e.g., Expo/FCM/APNs.
  • Support tools — ticketing/messaging if used ([specify]).
  • Voluntary sharing — with partners you invite inside Waddle (shared spaces/relations).
  • Authorities — where required by law.

Our processors act under Waddle’s instructions under GDPR-compliant agreements.

7. International transfers

Some data may be processed outside the EU/EEA (e.g., the United States). Where applicable, we implement appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and additional measures as needed.

8. Retention

  • Account & content: for the time you use the service, then deletion or anonymization within a reasonable period after closure.
  • Logs & security: short terms necessary for diagnostics (typically 3–12 months).
  • Subscriptions: records retained as required by law (e.g., accounting/tax).
  • Support: for the time needed to handle your request, then limited archiving.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, access controls, backups, logging, secure development). No system is infallible; please also protect your device and credentials.

10. Your rights (GDPR)

You may exercise: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and where applicable, withdraw consent. Contact us at support@waddle.app and include your request and the email tied to your account.

You may also set instructions for your data after death where local law provides.

11. Cookies & trackers

Mobile app: no browser cookies, but technical identifiers may be used for login, security, notifications, or usage measurement (per Apple/Google rules). Manage certain preferences in your OS (notifications, ad identifier).

Website: if analytics/audience cookies are used, they have limited durations and, where required, rely on your consent via a preference banner. You can change your choices at any time ([link to cookie preferences center]).

12. Children

Waddle is not directed to children below the age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect children’s data without appropriate consent. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us for deletion.

13. Changes

We may update this Policy for legal, technical, or operational reasons. For material changes, we will provide appropriate notice. The “Last updated” date above reflects the current version.

14. Contact & complaints

Contact: contact@waddle.fr

Supervisory authority: you may lodge a complaint with the CNIL (France) or your local data protection authority.

Transparency: This Policy specifically covers Firebase, RevenueCat, App Store / Google Play billing, push notifications, and analytics to satisfy platform and GDPR requirements.
